Our Vibe

Nestled in the heart of Seoul, Meatball Lounge x Analog Kitchen blends modern sophistication with cozy comfort. Our space features warm lighting, exposed brick walls, and analog-inspired decor that creates an inviting atmosphere for any occasion.

We specialize in innovative meatball fusion dishes that combine traditional Korean flavors with global culinary techniques. Each meatball is handcrafted with premium ingredients and served with creative accompaniments that elevate this humble comfort food to an art form.

Discover our signature pasta dish, crafted with care and premium ingredients.

Meatball Pasta

Succulent meatballs nestled in perfectly al dente pasta, smothered in our signature creamy sauce and finished with a generous sprinkle of aged parmesan. A comforting classic with a gourmet twist.

Meatball Platter

Meatball & French fries (Tomato or Demiglace or Jalapeno cream). Crispy fries paired with juicy meatballs, served with your choice of sauce for the perfect comfort food snack or meal.

Mushroom Cream Risotto

A rich and velvety Italian-style risotto, made with creamy Arborio rice, sautéed mushrooms, and a touch of parmesan for a deep, earthy flavor in every bite.
